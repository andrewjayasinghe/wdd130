Overview

Purpose

My plan is to create a website for a recreational fish breeding / export company. This site will contain a portfolio of fish that the exporter breeds including a description and pictures. In addition to this, the site will contain a home, about, products and a Contact page. Each page will contain information relevant to their titles

Audience

The target audience for my website will be small to large businesses that are interested in recreational fish sales. These can include major stores such as pet smart or more localized vendors that import recreational fish from outside of their home countries.

Content

Home page

Sri Lanka is an island nation located in the Indian ocean between latitudes 5⁰ 55’ to 9⁰ 51’ N and longitudes 79⁰ 41’ to 81⁰ 53’ E. It is a nation blessed with Blue Mountains, natural forests, ample rainfall, mild tropical temperatures, wild life, flora and fauna and is a tropical paradise which is home to many species of indigenous aquatic life. As of late, the raising of many varieties of ornamental fish for the domestic hobby market and now, more than ever, for the global export market is emerging as a new business trend. Over 90% of our exports amounts to the live bearer category and Nikoi Aqua is emerging as a reckonable force in the industry which is passionate about the high quality and uniqueness of its product range. You will realize the validity of this statement as you browse our website and product catalogue.

Images for the Home page


About

We are a subsidiary of Thalagala Holdings incorporated and are based in Burnaby, Canada with extensive operations in UK, USA, Australia, Germany and Asia. Our specialty is in breeding and growing a wide range of tropical ornamental fish varieties at our farm in Sri Lanka. The quality concept of the company is the belief in the proficiency of our workforce to deliver superior quality products meeting international standards. At present, the company exports guppies, mollies, sword tails, angels, barbs & betta to multiple international destinations. The extensive R & D effort of the company which produces new varieties continually is the niche of the company. You are bound to be surprised by new and exciting products, only Nikoi Aqua can supply.

Products

The Catalogue is as follows.

Guppies - Poecilia Reticulata Hailing from the Poecilia Reticulata family and originating in Trinidad, Guppies have come through generations of evolution. Guppy is an ornamental aquatic variety of livebearer category which is being exported in high volumes from Sri Lanka. Nikoi Aqua, has some of the unique and extremely rare varieties of guppies in our stocks conditioned for the niche export market segment for those that appreciate the quality and uniqueness of their aquariums. The specific body details indicated beneath each guppy variety has come about into existence owing to the sharpness in selecting the brood stocks, breeding techniques, water quality management, nutrition and other environmental conditions. Our aquatic stocks will always deliver the ultimate satisfaction in aquarium keeping hobby inducing a therapeutic meditation to the soul.
