About Us

We are incorporated and are based in Burnaby, Canada with our farms located in Sri Lanka. Our specialty is in breeding and growing a wide range of tropical ornamental fish varieties at our farm. The quality concept of the company is the belief in the proficiency of our workforce to deliver superior quality products meeting international standards. At present, the company exports guppies, mollies, sword tails, angels, barbs & betta to multiple international destinations. The extensive R & D effort of the company which produces new varieties continually is the niche of the company. You are bound to be surprised by new and exciting products, only Nikoi Aqua can supply.
