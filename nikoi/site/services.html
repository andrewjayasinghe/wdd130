Guppies - Poecilia Reticulata

Hailing from the Poecilia Reticulata family and originating in Trinidad, Guppies have come through generations of evolution. Guppy is an ornamental aquatic variety of livebearer category which is being exported in high volumes from Sri Lanka. Nikoi Aqua, has some of the unique and extremely rare varieties of guppies in our stocks conditioned for the niche export market segment for those that appreciate the quality and uniqueness of their aquariums.

The specific body details indicated beneath each guppy variety has come about into existence owing to the sharpness in selecting the brood stocks, breeding techniques, water quality management, nutrition and other environmental conditions. Our aquatic stocks will always deliver the ultimate satisfaction in aquarium keeping hobby inducing a therapeutic meditation to the soul.

Short body fish. Platinum front body with black eye and black rear

Yellow body with dark yellow cuddle and dorsal. Tuxedo pattern middle body

Brown head with pink belly. This breed is only available with us

Unique embossed red colour patches in front and rear with black cobra scale type middle body
